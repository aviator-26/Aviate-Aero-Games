This Privacy Policy applies to:

3. What Data Is Collected

We do not require you to create an account and we do not directly collect information such as your name, address, or phone number through the App.

However, the App uses third-party services that may automatically collect certain data for technical, advertising, and analytical purposes.

3.1 Data Collected Automatically

Depending on your device, settings, and consent choices, the following categories of data may be processed:

  • Device and technical information (such as device model, operating system version)
  • IP address (which may be anonymized or truncated)
  • Approximate location (country or region level only)
  • App usage and interaction data (such as session duration, crashes, or basic usage events)
  • Advertising Identifiers: We process unique device identifiers for advertising purposes. This includes the Android Advertising ID (AAID) on Android devices and the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) on iOS devices. These identifiers are used to deliver personalized ads and to track the effectiveness of ad campaigns.

The App uses Apple Game Center and Google Play Games Services for leaderboard functionality. These services process your player identity (display name and player ID) to display and rank scores. This data is managed by Apple and Google respectively.

We do not attempt to combine this data with other sources to identify you personally.

3.2 In-Game Economy and Rewards

To manage your virtual currency ('Coins') and award coins for viewing rewarded advertisements, the App uses local storage and technical identifiers. This data ensures that your coin balance is correctly updated and maintained across sessions. We do not link this information to your real-world identity.

The App offers optional in-app purchases ('tips') on iOS, processed entirely by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information. Purchase history is managed by Apple according to their privacy policy.

4. How Data Is Used

Data collected through the App may be used for the following purposes:

  • Displaying advertisements
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Preventing fraud and abuse
  • Improving app stability and performance

We do not sell personal data.

The App displays advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners, such as Google AdMob.

For users located in the European Union (EU), European Economic Area (EEA), or the United Kingdom:

  • Personalized advertising is shown only if you give explicit consent.
  • If you do not give consent, or if you later withdraw consent, the App will display non-personalized advertisements.

Ad partners may use technologies such as cookies, SDKs, or device identifiers to deliver ads and measure their performance, in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable law.

On iOS devices, the App will request your permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites before displaying personalized ads. If you decline, only non-personalized ads will be shown. You can change this setting at any time in your device's Settings under Privacy & Tracking.

You may withdraw your consent at any time through the App's privacy or consent settings. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.
